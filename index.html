Designers need to master prompt engineering and contextual logic, ensuring generated UIs remain consistent with brand and user needs.

Researching Adaptive AI: New UX Methods

Traditional UX research methods are adapting to study AI's dynamic and probabilistic nature. Emerging techniques include prompt engineering research, contextual observation of AI-human interactions, and longitudinal studies tracking adaptive system behavior. Understanding user trust and mental models is key.

Researching AI isn't just about 'what works'; it's about 'how users adapt to and trust systems that continuously learn.'

Explore new research methodologies to effectively gather insights on user interactions with adaptive and intelligent systems.
