Inclusive AI: Designing for Accessibility in Intelligent Products

AI has the potential to both enhance and hinder accessibility. Designers must proactively ensure AI-powered features, like smart assistants or predictive text, are built with inclusive principles, addressing diverse user needs and avoiding algorithmic biases that could exclude users.

True innovation in AI means building experiences that empower everyone, breaking down barriers rather than creating new ones.

Prioritize accessibility from conception, test AI features with diverse user groups, and advocate for inclusive data sets.
